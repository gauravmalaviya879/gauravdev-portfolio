About Happy

Happy Media is an Australian based youth culture publisher focussed primarily on independent news, reviews, video content, and feature articles. After starting as a weekly gig six years ago, Happy has grown into an influential content provider for millions of people in Australia and beyond.

Considered a leading tastemaker in the emerging music and arts space, Happy’s editorial team is made up of equal parts active contributors in the creative community (from community radio hosts to artist managers and musicians) as well as young journalists and established writers.

We are passionate supporters and participants in the spaces we choose to represent, from music to gaming and the arts. A member of the Happy Media editorial team is an active musician, a knowledgeable audio engineer, a die-hard gaming fan, or an active voice in their niche community – whatever it may be. If we’re writing about it, we love it, and you can bet we’ve got the inside scoop.

Happy’s content is authentic and inclusive, sitting firmly outside of any elitist discourse. Every day we aim to entertain, educate, and provoke critical conversations through our highly curated written and video content spanning world news, literature, pop culture, music, gaming, and the arts.
