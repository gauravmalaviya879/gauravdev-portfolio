Destroyer- Tintoretto, It’s for You

Buzz has released this incredible emotive and borderline acapella song. The single is a whirlwind of layered vocals, giving us Bon Iver meets Imogen Heap vibes. Buzz said she wrote the song mid spiral and that it’s helped her ever since, we can see why.
